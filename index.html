FAQ

What is a percentage?

A percentage is a way to express a number as a portion of 100. It helps you understand how one quantity relates to another in a simple, standardized format. For instance, if there are 200 books on a shelf and 50 are blue, then 25% of the books are blue.

How do you calculate a percentage?

To calculate a percentage, divide the part by the total amount, then multiply by 100. For example, if you have 60 oranges and 15 are ripe, dividing 15 by 60 gives 0.25. Multiply by 100, and you find that 25% of the oranges are ripe.

Why percentages are important

Percentages are everywhere from shopping discounts and tax rates to investment growth and performance metrics. Understanding percentages helps you make informed decisions, plan your finances, and evaluate changes in value accurately, whether at work or in daily life.
